Dominion Stories

We all love stories, from the curious infant yearning for a good tale to the frail elders with stories for a lifetime, spanning fiction, true events or a bit of folklore. The Almighty God is the best-storyteller in history, because he has been before the beginning of time and has existed through all ages to give us the most accurate details at every point in the time continuum. He doesn't just give stories, he changes them at his will, he rewrites history over and over for just the one man. We urge you to take a look at some of our stories of miracles, signs, wonders and changes happening in our small part of the world, and we in turn hope that your faith becomes woke as a result to know that God is able to do exceeding abundantly above all we could ever ask or think.
